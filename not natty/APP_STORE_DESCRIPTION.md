# iOS-Compliant Supplement Tracking App Specification

## Overview
This app is designed for bodybuilders, gym enthusiasts, and athletes to track **legal, over-the-counter supplements**.
It replaces any references to controlled or prescription substances with safe, compliant supplement options.
The app provides users with **daily dosage tracking, weekly summaries, and usage analytics**.

## Core Features

### 1. Daily Dosage Tracking
- Users can log supplement names, dosages, and times taken.
- Dosage units can be grams, milligrams, or servings.

### 2. Weekly Summary Reports
- Weekly breakdown of total supplements consumed.
- Visual charts for trends over time.
- Ability to export reports as PDF.

### 3. Supplement History
- Timeline of past supplement logs.
- Filters for specific supplements.

### 4. Reminders & Notifications
- Optional daily reminders to log supplements.
- Push notifications for scheduled doses.

### 5. Custom Supplement Creation
- Users can add their own supplements not listed in the database.
- Ability to choose category and dosage units.

---

## iOS Compliance Strategy
- No mention of controlled substances in marketing or UI.
- All supplement names are over-the-counter and legal.
- App Store Connect settings will ensure availability in all legal markets.
